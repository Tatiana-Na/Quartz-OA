Why Quartz

Quartz is a cheap and widely available material. We use it metaphorically for our community currency just as other cheap materials like paper are used to represent currencies. Quartz is also a hard material that can scratch gold. Likewise, Quartz OA can scratch Gold Open Access prices, and perforate holes to access paywalled academic content for a fraction of the current price.
